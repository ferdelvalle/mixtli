The Survey

In order to gather information, a survey was designed with four parts:

  1. General diagnosis

  2. IT diagnosis

  3. IT usage diagnosis

  4. Cloud computing knowledge diagnosis

1 General diagnosis

Aims to gather general information about the organization.

  • To which industry does your organization belong to?

  • How many employees work at your organization?

  • Does your organization have a presence in more than one location?

  • In which country is your organization’s main place of business located?

  • In which state is your organization's main place of business located? (Available only for México)

  • Does your organization have clients or providers in other cities, estates or countries?

2 IT diagnosis

Aim's to find out what kind of IT does the organization use.

  • Does your organization use computers?

  • Is the internet used for any, or all, tasks within your organization?

  • Does your organization have a dedicated IT department, or person?

3 IT usage diagnosis

Aims to know how information is managed and shared.

  • Which of the following pieces of software are used within your organization?

  • Within your organization, where the documents are saved to?

  • Do you know if a server, of any kind, is used by your organization?

  • When you want to share a file with another member of your organization, what do you use?

  • Are you aware of the risks involved with the different ways of sharing files with other members of your organization?

  • Does your organization have any policies or rules regarding data security?

4 Cloud computing knowledge diagnosis

Aims to measure awareness of Cloud Computing.

  • Do you know what Cloud Computing is?

  • Have you ever heard of AWS of Azure?

  • Does your organization currently use any cloud computing service, or does it have plans to use any within 2021?

  • Where would you consider the safest place to store your information?

  • For an organization, it is less expensive to use a cloud computing service than to use their own servers.

  • For an organization, it is safer to use a cloud computing service than to use their own servers.

  • Using a cloud computing service is a lot more difficult than using servers owned by the organization.
